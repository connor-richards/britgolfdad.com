How do I schedule a live lesson?

If you are in the Sacramento Area of California we can easily set up a time for your lesson! Email me at britgolfdad@gmail.com and we can come up with a time that would work for the both of us. I do my lessons at Catta Verdera Country Club in Lincoln, CA, generally Tuesdays through Fridays 8am to 2pm, but I can be flexible!

How do I schedule an online lesson?

There are several ways we can do a video chat lesson, but I typically use Zoom or WhatsApp. Email me at britgolfdad@gmail.com and we can come up with a time that would work for the both of us. I am in California, so we'll have to come up with a time that works for both of us if you are in a different timezone. You'll have to have a place where your camera can view your entire swing and you have a reliable internet connection.
